About Ushodaya Health Care Charity Trust

Ushodaya Health Care Charity Trust has been dedicated to providing free and affordable eye care services since 2013. With the support of kind-hearted donors and skilled professionals, we conduct vision camps, surgeries, and awareness programs for underprivileged communities across Telangana.

  • Free Eye Checkups & Surgeries
  • Rural & School Vision Camps
  • Supported by Donors & Volunteers
  • Vision Awareness Programs
Learn More
Our Eye Care Services

Dedicated Eye Care For the Underprivileged

Free Eye Checkups

Regular vision screenings and checkups conducted at our trust centers and camps for people who cannot afford them.

Free Cataract Surgeries

Life-changing cataract surgeries for elderly patients in rural areas who otherwise can't access treatment.

Laser Eye Treatments

Advanced treatments including laser therapy for various eye conditions at zero cost for underprivileged patients.

School Vision Camps

Free eye screening camps for school children to detect early vision problems and prevent learning issues.

Donor-Sponsored Care

Every surgery and checkup is made possible by kind donors — sponsor a surgery or support a camp today.

Vision Awareness Programs

Community education programs to raise awareness on eye health, hygiene, and preventable blindness.
