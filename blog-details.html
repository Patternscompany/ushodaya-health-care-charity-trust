5th August 2025 Rural Telangana

Free Eye Screening & Spectacle Distribution

In our recent rural health camp in nuthankal village, Suryapet district, Telangana over 1850+ people were screened for vision issues and 956+ individuals received free spectacles and 470 free cataract surgeries performed. This initiative focused on delivering vital eye care to underprivileged communities, helping them regain clear vision and confidence in daily life.

  • 1850+ People Screened
  • 956+ Received Free Spectacles
  • 470+ Cataract Surgeries
  • Focus on Rural Eye Care
  • Promoting Vision Health Awareness
Ongoing Service Rural Telangana

Free Surgeries & Medication Support

At Ushodaya Health Care Charity Trust, we provide free eye surgeries and essential medications for people living in rural areas who cannot afford the cost of treatment.

In many villages, people are unaware of the importance of eye care and often suffer silently due to lack of awareness and financial support. Our trust steps in to fill this gap — offering medical care, surgeries, and follow-up support at no cost to the patient.

  • 100% Free Eye Surgeries
  • Medication Provided at No Cost
  • Special Focus on Rural Communities
  • Spreading Awareness on Eye Health
